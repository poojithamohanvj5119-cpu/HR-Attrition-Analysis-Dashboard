# HR-Attrition-Analysis-Dashboard
## Project Overview
The HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard is an intractive Power BI project developed to analyze employee attrition patterns and support HR decision-making. The Dashboard provides insights into employee turnover by department,overtime,job satisfaction, and years at company.

## Business Objective 
The objective of this project is to:
-Monitor employee attrition trends.
-Identify department with higher attrition.
-Anlyze the impact of overtime and job satisfaction.
-Support HR teams with data-driven insights for employee retention.

## Tools Used

-Power BI
-SQL
-Microsoft Excel
-DAX
-Power Query

## Dashboard Features

-Total Employees
-Attrition Count
-Attrition Rate
-Average Monthly Income
-Average Years at Company
-Attrition by Department
-Attrition by Overtime
-Attrition by Job Satisfaction
-Attrition Trends by Years at Company
-Intreactive Slicers

## Key Insights

-Sales department has the highest attrition rate.
-Employees working overtime has a higher attrition rate.
-Lower job satisfaction is associated with increased attrition.
-Employee tenure influences attrition trends.
